Travel to New Delhi!

New Delhi is an amazing place!
It has a lot of places like:

Jantar Mantar

  • The Jantar Mantar is a monument in the old Delhi Area
  • It is a clock that used shadows to keep time.

India Gate

  • The India Gate is a war memorial to the fallen Indian Soldiers in World War I.
  • It was built by the British Raj in India.

Bangla Sahib

  • The Bangla Sahib is the main Gurudwara in New Delhi
  • A gurudwara is a temple for the Sikh religion.
